What is BDT?

The Business Data Technologies (BDT) organization exists to serve Amazon's growing analytics needs. The BDT teams develop and evolve services for storage and access to the authoritative repository of all data published by source teams (teams that create or own original data) and enhanced with aggregations and transformations for use by consuming teams across Amazon, using modern compute services.

What we've built

We have evolved our services from the legacy Oracle compute and storage infrastructure with a number of new concepts which extend analytics capabilities. See the full list of our products and services.

Mission

BDT’s mission is to accelerate Amazon’s data-driven business, enable the next generation of analytics and machine learning technologies at scale, and raise the bar on global customer trust by cataloging, protecting, enriching, and brokering data through its lifecycle.
